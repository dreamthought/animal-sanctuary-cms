The Animal Sanctuary is closed to the general public. We are a 100% volunteer-run sanctuary, and our whole day is spent working, feeding and caring for the animals and native birds. We are open for visits from our wonderful Sanctuary Supporters by prior arrangement. ðŸ˜Š

The Animal Sanctuary is an animal refuge run by Shawn Bishop and Michael Dixon in Matakana, New Zealand. We provide a safe haven for abused and neglected animals, and care for injured or orphaned native birds. A charitable trust has been established for this purpose.
