We welcome donations for our Animals Op Shop Store

Thank you for your generous offer to donate a quality item to list on Trade Me for our “Animals Op Shop” store! The proceeds from this sale will help us continue the important work we do, and we couldn’t do it without support from people like you.

To help us create the best listing possible, please complete this form and send it back to us at animalsopshop@gmail.com with at least three photographs of the item. We suggest you follow these guidelines for photos:

  • Use high-resolution, or detailed photographs that clearly show the item details
  • Try and have the item be the only thing in the picture, or taking up as much of the picture as possible so its clear what item is for sale
  • Take your photos in bright or natural light and avoid shadows
  • Landscape (horizontal) photos work best
  • Photograph smaller items on a white, light background that’s free of distractions
  • Include photos from multiple angles, including closeups of any imperfections, brand names, or unique features
Tips: • Include size/measurements • Note unique or special features that make this item stand out • Include details or descriptions that you as a buyer would like to know about • Note any imperfections or damage
