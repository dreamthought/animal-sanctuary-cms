COW CARE - a new option to save lives

Rescued cows finally have an option. If we find them a sponsor, they can go to Cow Care to live out their full life. Cow Care provides all the facilities to care for cows for the rest of their lives: beautiful grazing on a huge property, they're moved to fresh grass every day, weighed regularly, checked on daily, given supplements, baylage in winter if necessary, annual vaccination, etc. etc. etc.

The cost to sponsor a cow is $12 +GST per week. That's less than some places charge just for grazing, with no individual care for the animals. As a sponsor you can visit your cow; receive reports, photos & updates about your cow; join our private FB sponsors page; receive invites to private Cow Care functions such as picnics, etc.

If you're interested, I'd love to chat with you to provide the full story, and answer any questions. Please CONTACT US with your name and phone number, and I'll give you a call. Thank you. (Note, if you're a private owner that wants to place your own cows at Cow Care, that's great! Let me know that and I'll send you a flyer.)
