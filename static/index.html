WELCOME TO THE ANIMAL SANCTUARY

The Animal Sanctuary is run by Shawn Bishop and Michael Dixon based in Matakana, New Zealand. We provide a safe haven for abused and neglected animals, and care for injured and orphaned native birds.

Our 32 acre property has grazing land to permanently home abused animals, and is bordered by 400 acres of protected bush where we can safely release rehabilitated native birds.

We currently care for around 100 animals in The Sanctuary: two-legged and four-legged, native and exotic, warm and cold-blooded – all getting the care that they deserve.

You can watch our video to learn more about what we do, and Follow us on Facebook to see what we get up to.

The Animal Sanctuary is a haven for all animals, with a focus on three main areas of work:

Native Birds

We rehabilitate and release native New Zealand birds back into the wild.
